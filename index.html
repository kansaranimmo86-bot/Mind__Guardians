About MindGuardians

We are students helping students with mental health challenges

Why We Started

College life can be tough. Between exams, assignments, and being away from home, many students feel stressed and alone.

We created MindGuardians to provide a safe space where students can get mental health support from people who understand what they're going through.

Our team includes fellow students and mental health professionals who work together to help you feel better.
